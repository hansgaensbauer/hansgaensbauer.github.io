The HAALO Balloon

After months of design and prototyping, East High School Engineering brings you HAALO: the High Altitude Atmosphere and Land Observatory. With a target altitude of 100,000ft, where temperatures sink below -51.1 degrees Fahrenheit, every aspect of this balloon has been built for durability and resilience.
